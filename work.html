Samples:

An FEA model of acrylic clips for engaging a vacuum to the spindle housing of the deskopt CNC mills. There was an ongoing issue of these clips snapping at the thin sections. This model allowed me to adjust the geometry to increase the strength by 50% and remove the issue for customers.

Another FEA model for a custom designed spindle. I created this model for our R&D team to better understand the potential for breakage under high loads of torque while cutting aluminum.
